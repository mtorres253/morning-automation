# Lambda System Issues — RESOLVED — April 16, 2026

## The Problems & Solutions

### Problem 1: Job Search Arriving Twice Daily ✅ FIXED

**What was happening:**
- 9 AM: `morning-automation-rule` triggered job-search-lambda
- 10 AM: `job-search-daily` rule triggered job-search-lambda again (duplicate!)

**Root cause:**
- Two separate EventBridge rules both targeting the same Lambda
- `job-search-daily` was created accidentally and never deleted

**Solution:**
- Deleted `job-search-daily` rule (kept `morning-automation-rule`)
- Now only one job-search execution per day at 9 AM ✅

**Timeline:**
- Created April 9-10, 2026 (when deploy.py ran)
- Deleted April 16, 2026 at 9:57 AM
